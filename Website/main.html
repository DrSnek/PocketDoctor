Intro

This is a project made by the the Pathfinding Pioneers. We are a group of 11th grade students from Greenwoodhigh International School on a mission to innovate the healthcare sector. Our is focused on increasing the reach of standardized healthcare to rural areas at minimal costs.

The Algorithm

The machine learning algorithm developed by the team aims to identify the risks of a person having a certain disease based on mutiple datasets. The ML algorithm processes and then compares the data to obtain a final result.
